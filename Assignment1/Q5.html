Wings of Fire is an autobiographical book by Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the former President of India.
It chronicles his early life, childhood, education, and experiences leading up to his career as a scientist.
The book provides insight into his journey from a small town in Tamil Nadu to becoming a key figure in India's space and missile programs.
It highlights the values of hard work, perseverance, and determination, and serves as an inspiring narrative for young readers.
The book also touches on his vision for India's future and his experiences working with leaders and mentors.
